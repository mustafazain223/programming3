Climate Care in Egypt: Challenges and Improvements In recent years, Egypt has undertaken several plans and programs to deal with expected climate changes. In the agricultural field, the government has used techniques such as irrigation and artificial forests to improve water conservation rates and dry feeding. The government also supports local farming families with training and financial aid to teach them how to deal with climate change. In the public sphere, the government also supports training programs aimed at improving people's knowledge of climate change and how to combat it. It also supports small and medium enterprises that aim to reduce the consumption of natural resources and reduce the environmental burden
Egypt is a country located in the northeast of the continent of Africa and is known to suffer from severe climatic changes. Moreover, Egypt suffers from the hot, humid and dry weather, which causes a significant deterioration in the climate as well as in agriculture. Moreover, Egypt suffers from major floods in the current season, and this causes collapses in rivers and deep rivers, which cause major problems in the country. Moreover, Egypt suffers from environmental degradation as well as from environmental pollution that leads to the accumulation of dust and polluted air. Therefore, there are many climatic changes that Egypt suffers from that need to be resolved to improve the climatic conditions in the country.
Cop 27 is a social organized by the United Nations Climate Organization (UNFCCC) in 2021 in Egypt. Its goal is cooperation between countries to overcome climate change and achieve sustainable development. Cop 27 is important because it deals with the increasing climate change we're seeing in the world today. Cooperative international action is the only way to address this crucial issue. At Cop 27, countries discussed actions that can help deal with climate change and achieve sustainable development. A global plan for cooperation in this field has also been defined, and cooperation has been made with the concerned special forces to support countries in implementing this plan. In addition, Cop 27 has supported countries suffering severe impacts from climate change
One of the pioneering projects in Egypt to face climate change is the solar energy project. This project aims to achieve international cooperation to develop and expand the use of solar energy in Egypt. This project is considered one of the most important alternative energy projects in the world, and it is considered one of the most invested alternative energy projects in the world. The solar energy project in Egypt is one of the projects that will help reduce the consumption of oil and water energy, and in dealing with the climate changes that occur in the country. Solar energy can be a source of electrical energy in areas that need this type of energy.
Egypt launched the National Climate Change Strategy 2050 on May 19, 2022, on the sidelines of its participation in the activities of the Climate Conference in Glasgow, "COP 26". The strategy will enable Egypt to plan and manage climate change at different levels and support the achievement of sustainable development goals and the goals of Egypt's Vision 2030 by adopting a flexible and low-emissions approach. The objectives of the strategy are to achieve sustainable economic growth through low-emissions development in various sectors, by increasing the share of renewable and alternative energy sources in the energy mix, and maximizing energy efficiency.
The Egyptian strategy in the field of new and renewable energy sources aims to provide 5% of the country's total basic needs through new and renewable energy sources, with priority given to wind energy, solar energy and bioenergy. .
